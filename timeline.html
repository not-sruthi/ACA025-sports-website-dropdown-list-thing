A brief look at the life of
Vincent Zhao

  • 2000 Vincent Zhao was born.
  • 2005 Zhao begins ice skating.
  • 2009 Zhao becomes a member of the All Year Figure Skating Club.
  • 2011 Zhao becomes the youngest U.S. intermediate champion.
  • 2012 Zhao becomes the U.S. novice champion.
  • 2013 Zhao becomes the youngest U.S. junior champion.
  • 2013-15 Zhao misses the skating season due to injuries.
  • 2015 Zhou begins training at the Broadmoor Skating Club in Colorado Springs.
  • 2015-16 Zhao places 4th in JGP final in Barcelona.
  • 2016 Zhao places 8th at Senior level at the US Championships.
  • 2016 Zhao wins silver in the Junior Grand Prix series.
  • 2017 Zhao wins silver in the US Championships at the Senior Level.
  • 2017 Zhao wins the World Junior Championships.
  • 2017 Zhao places 2nd in Finlandia Trophy.
  • 2018 Zhao wins bronze in U.S. Figure Skating Championships.
  • 2018 Zhao places 4th in CS U.S. International Classic.
  • 2019 Zhao wins silver in US Championships.
  • 2019 Zhao wins bronze in Four Continents Championships.
  • 2019 Zhao wins bronze in World Championships.
  • 2019 Zhao wins bronze in CS U.S. Classic.
  • 2020 Zhao places 4th in U.S. Championships.
  • 2020 Zhao wins silver in Skate America.
